    Armed with the mission of becoming the country’s leader in cosmetics, British Cosmetics counts on 19 years of hands-on experience in the industry and a vision to provide uncompromised world class skin and beauty care products to an esteemed client base.

    A high level of respect and care for the consumers and their wellbeing has been the guiding principle and formula for success at British Cosmetics and the company to date stands committed to providing the best possible solutions to the Sri Lankan market.

    Led by Dimuth Jagodaarachchi & Nirosha Jagodaarachchi, the experienced professionals with a wealth of experience in the industry, the British Cosmetics team is one that values integrity and professionalism above all else. British Cosmetics is committed to furthering the success story of the Sri Lankan cosmetics industry, scaling new heights of personal beauty care and lifestyle.
